Privacy Policy for Tic-Tac-Toe

Last updated: December 22, 2025

Overview

Tic-Tac-Toe ("the App") is a simple game application that respects your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information.

Information Collection

We do not collect any personal information.

The App does not:

  • Collect any personal data
  • Track your activity
  • Use cookies or similar technologies
  • Require registration or login
  • Access your contacts, photos, or other personal files
  • Share any data with third parties

Data Storage

The App works completely offline. All game data stays on your device and is never transmitted to external servers. Your game preferences and settings are stored locally on your device only.

Children's Privacy

The App is safe for children of all ages. Since we do not collect any information, there is no risk to children's privacy.

Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party services, analytics, or advertising networks.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
